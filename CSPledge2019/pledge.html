Kentucky Computer Science Pledge

Computer Science for Kentucky (CSforKY), started out as KentuckyCoders, an initiative to get one million citizens of the Bluegrass coding. Inspired by all the programs worldwide working to bring computer science to students in grades K-12, KentuckyCoders has transformed into CSforKY, whose mission is to ensure that all Kentucky students have the opportunity to learn Computer Science in school.
